Featured Dish

Fish Amok

Fish Amok is one of Cambodia’s most famous dishes. It is a rich and creamy curry made with coconut milk, spices, and fresh fish, then steamed in banana leaves.

This dish represents Khmer culture and is loved for its soft texture and unique flavour.

Why Try This Dish?

  • Rich and unique flavour
  • Traditional Cambodian recipe
  • Healthy ingredients
  • Popular among locals and tourists
