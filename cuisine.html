Khmer Cuisine

Explore some of the most popular Cambodian dishes.

Fish Amok

A traditional Cambodian curry made with fish, coconut milk, and spices. It is steamed in banana leaves, giving it a soft texture and rich flavour.

Beef Lok Lak

A stir-fried beef dish served with rice, fresh vegetables, and a tangy dipping sauce. It has a savoury flavour and is a well-known Khmer favourite.

Phnom Penh Noodles

A popular Cambodian noodle soup made with rice noodles, clear broth, herbs, and meat or seafood. It is light, fresh, and commonly eaten for breakfast.

Num Banh Chok

A classic Khmer breakfast dish made with fresh rice noodles and a light green fish curry gravy. It is served with herbs and fresh vegetables for a refreshing taste.

Samlor Machu

A traditional sour Cambodian soup made with vegetables, herbs, and meat or fish. It has a tangy taste and is often eaten as a comforting everyday meal.

Kampot Pepper Crab

A famous seafood dish from Kampot made with fresh crab cooked in a rich sauce using local Kampot pepper. It is spicy, aromatic, and very flavourful.
